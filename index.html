Guide

Step-by-Step

1

Open the Review Queue

The queue shows all available calls filtered by your role. Leaders see all reps; reps see their own calls for peer review. Use column filters to narrow by account, rep, stage, date, or duration.

2

Select a Call to Review

Click any row to open the call workspace. You'll see the embedded Gong player on the left and the review form on the right. Collapsible call details and AI summary sections appear above.

3

Listen and Score

Play the Gong recording while filling out the review form. Score each of the 9 STC categories from 0–5 and optionally add comments. The overall score calculates automatically as the average.

4

Submit Your Review

Hit submit. Your review is saved to the AppDB collection and automatically synced to the dashboard dataset. A success toast confirms the submission and you return to the queue.

5

Check the Dashboard

Switch to the User Dashboard to see aggregated scores, KPI cards for each STC category, score trends over time, and the Top Performers section. Filter by rep, account, date range, or review type.

Best Practices

Tips for Great Reviews

Be Specific in Comments

Instead of "good job," note the exact moment: "At 4:32, the rep asked a strong discovery question about budget timeline that revealed the real decision-maker."

Calibrate with the Team

Review a few calls as a group first to align on what a 3 vs. 5 looks like for each category. Top Performers shows high-scoring calls as reference.

Use the Reviewed Calls Tab

After submitting, switch to "Reviewed Calls" to see all your past reviews. Sort by score to find patterns and ensure consistency.

Check AI Reviews First

AI reviews run daily on every call. Before starting your own review, check if an AI score exists — it can help you calibrate.

Gong Login Required

The embedded Gong player requires you to be logged into Gong via Okta SSO. If the player shows a login prompt, click the login button — the app will automatically reload the player when you return.

Scoring Framework

The 9 STC Categories

Measure the complete sales conversation, from preparation to demo execution.

Category 1
Current Situation
Did the rep understand where the prospect is today?
Category 2
Call Preparedness
Was the rep prepared with research and an agenda?
Category 3
Problem Defined
Did the rep clearly identify the prospect's pain points?
Category 4
Impact
Was the business impact of the problem articulated?
Category 5
Ideal
Did the rep paint a picture of the ideal future state?
Category 6
Benefit
Were specific, quantifiable benefits discussed?
Category 7
Layering
Did the rep layer questions to deepen understanding?
Category 8
Demo Preparedness
Was the demo tailored to the prospect's specific needs?
Category 9
Quantified Value
Did the rep tie Domo's value to measurable outcomes?

Scoring Scale: 0 – 5

Each category is scored from 0 to 5. The overall score is the average of all 9 categories. Scores below 3.0 are flagged for coaching, 3.0–3.9 indicate solid performance, and 4.0+ represents excellence.
